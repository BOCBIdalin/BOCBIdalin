About Me

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Xi'an Jiaotong University.

During my graduate studies, I spent one year as an exchange student at Politecnico di Milano, which gave me a valuable opportunity to experience a different academic environment, meet people with diverse backgrounds, and think about research from a broader perspective.

This GitHub profile is a small place where I would like to record and share things that I find interesting, meaningful, or worth learning from.

Some of them may be related to research, papers, code, tools, or technical notes.
Some may simply come from curiosity, exploration, and daily study.

I hope this space can become a quiet but open corner for learning, communication, and mutual inspiration.
